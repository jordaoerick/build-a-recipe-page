Miojo (Noodles)

Here's a straightforward recipe for preparing instant noodles using only water and the seasoning packet provided

Ingredients

  • 1 package of instant noodles
  • 2 cups (approximately 480 ml) of water
  • Seasoning packet included with the noodles
  • Optional: Additional seasonings to taste (e.g., salt, pepper, garlic powder, chili flakes)

Instructions

  1. Boil the Water
  2. Add the Noodles
  3. Incorporate the Seasoning
  4. Add Additional Seasonings (Optional)
